BMS Technology

Battery Management System (BMS) technology is improving the performance, safety, and longevity of lithium batteries. BMS systems use complex algorithms to provide high-precision cell monitoring, active balancing, and accurate estimates of State-of-Charge (SoC) and State-of-Health (SoH).

They incorporate advanced safety features like overvoltage, overcurrent, and thermal runaway prevention, as well as dynamic thermal management. Advanced BMS also allows rapid charging, modular designs, and secure communication protocols such as CAN bus and wireless BMS.

Our lithium battery packs feature smart thermal management systems that balance performance, safety, and longevity, including:

  • High-conductivity thermal pads for rapid heat dissipation
